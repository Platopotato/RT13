About Radix Tribes

Radix Tribes is a turn-based, hex-map strategy game where players lead survivor factions in a devastated wasteland. Build garrisons, research forgotten technologies, trade (or wage war) with rival tribes, and survive long enough to dominate the new world.

How to Connect

To play Radix Tribes, you need to:
